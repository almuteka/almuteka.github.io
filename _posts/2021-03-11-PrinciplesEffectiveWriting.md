# Objectives
Technical writing for sciences can be a challenging task. A writer needs to deliver a message to readers in a direct, clear, and concise way. The point of writing a paper (e.g., tutorial or new findings) must be clear before the writing process. The objectives of this post are as follow:
1. Understand the principles of effective writing.
2. Learn the things a writer needs to avoid when writing.
3. How to be concise.
